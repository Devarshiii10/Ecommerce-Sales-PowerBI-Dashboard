## 📌 Problem Statement

An e-commerce company wants to analyze its sales performance across 
products, categories, and regions to identify top-performing products, 
understand customer buying patterns, and improve revenue strategies.

| Page | Name | Key Visuals |
|------|------|-------------|
| 1 | Sales Overview | KPI Cards, Monthly Trend, Order Status, Payment Method |

| 3 | Regional Analysis | Revenue by Region, Revenue by City |

---

## 🔢 Key KPIs Tracked

- Total Revenue
- Total Orders
- Total Profit
- Average Order Value (AOV)
- Profit Margin %
- Return Rate %
